What is premature ejaculation?

Premature ejaculation (PE) is a very common condition that affects about 1 in every 3 men.

Men with PE ejaculate sooner than they would like during sexual intercourse. Men with severe PE may ejaculate before penetration occurs.

Men with PE usually feel that they cannot control when they ejaculate and are bothered or distressed by this situation.
